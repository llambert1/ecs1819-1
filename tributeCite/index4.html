Tom Regan


Influencers

Immanuel Kant

Immanuel Kant was a German phisopher in the 18th century who is known for his work in ethics and metaphysics. Regan used and adapted Kant's ideas on Ethics in his work.

G. E. Moore

G. E. Moore was an English philospher in the 19th and 20th century who is known for greatly influencing meta-ethics and for the Moore's paradox; "it is raining but I do not belive it is raining". Moore was another philosopher that Regan used ideas of.

Peter Singer

Peter Singer was an Austrailian philosopher known for his work in ethics. He was a friend of Regan, and their ideas are very similar on the topic of animal rights.

Tom Regan Legacy

Tom Regan is famous for his animal rights and ethics beliefs. He beleived that all creatures must get the same basic rights that we give to humans and other superiorily intellegent animals. He argued that all living things have inherent value, and therefore must get proper treatment He also said some weird stuff about babies and the mentally impared.
